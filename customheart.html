Custom Heart Cosmetic

Being a LEM Standard Supporter and up, you are able to create a custom heart cosmetic to use in the server!

You first need to make the texture for your heart cosmetic, your texture must be 256x256 or lower in resolution (though we don't recommend going that high)

You may optionally make a dead variant of your texture, or you can just fallback to the built-in dead texture.

Suggestions

Here are also some suggestions for your texture:
- Keep the resolution 32x32 or lower (32x32 is still kind of pushing it)
- Try to stay in the style of the rest of the heart cosmetics
- Keep in mind that the tablist is tiny, stuff like text will not be readable unless its a single letter

These suggestions are not required to get your heart cosmetic approved, but you should probably follow them for it to look good next to everything else.

Download

This has a template heart cosmetic and a link to all heart cosmetics.
You can also take a look around the custom folder to see what other members have made for some examples of what you can do

Once you have finished your heart cosmetic, DM Emmie on Discord or Patreon about it to begin the upload & approval process
