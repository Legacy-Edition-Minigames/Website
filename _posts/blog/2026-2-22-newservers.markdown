  Hello! This is just a quick update to announce that we have more servers now!

  We used to have just 3 servers, now we have 6! 2 more stable servers, and one more experimental server.

  The names of the servers have been changed so we don't have "Battle Main Alternate Alternate Alternate" being a thing.

  The new server list looks like this:

  ### Stable
  - Battle 1 (16 players)
  - Battle 2 (16 players)
  - Battle 3 (16 players)
  - Battle 4 (8 players)

  ### Experimental
  - Experimental 1 (16 players)
  - Experimental 2 (8 players)
  - Europe (Temporarily disabled)

  You might of noticed, 2 of the servers have an 8 player cap, there have been requests for servers with a lower player cap due to 16 players feeling pretty chaotic. We plan to have a cleaner solution to this than just having 2 servers have a limit but, this is what we're doing for now until we can get there.

  We'll be back soon with some more news, for now have fun!
